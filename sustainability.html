Sustainability

Our commitment to eco-friendly practices and community support.

Sustainable Farming Practices

At Iriaini Tea Factory, we are committed to sustainable farming practices that protect the environment and ensure the long-term health of our tea plantations. Our farmers use organic methods, avoiding synthetic pesticides and fertilizers, to produce high-quality tea while preserving the natural ecosystem.

  • Use of organic compost and natural fertilizers.
  • Water conservation through efficient irrigation systems.
  • Promotion of biodiversity by planting shade trees and cover crops.

Environmental Impact

We are dedicated to minimizing our environmental footprint. Our factory uses renewable energy sources, recycles waste, and implements energy-efficient technologies to reduce greenhouse gas emissions and conserve natural resources.

  • 100% renewable energy usage in our processing facilities.
  • Recycling 80% of our waste annually.
  • Planting over 10,000 trees to support reforestation efforts.

Community Support

Sustainability is not just about the environment; it's also about supporting the communities that make our work possible. We provide fair wages, education programs, and healthcare initiatives to improve the quality of life for our farmers and their families.

  • Fairtrade practices ensuring fair prices for farmers.
  • Education and training programs for sustainable farming.
  • Healthcare initiatives to support local communities.

Iriaini Corporate Social Responsibility

Iriaini Supports Needy, Bright Students

Since 2021, Iriaini Tea Factory has supported needy but bright students by awarding one full secondary school scholarship per year to a deserving student from a disadvantaged background.

Iriaini Farmers Holistically and Economically Empowered

Through the HEE program, Iriaini has trained 250 farmers as Trainers of Trainers (TOT) in Financial Literacy, aiming to educate over 7,000 tea farmers on planning, spending, and investing for sustainability.
